# Clinic Go-Live in 7 Days (AER Adoption)

This plan assumes a single clinic pilot and a small initial cohort.

## Day 0: Setup
- Create clinic and staff accounts.
- Confirm timezone and clinic settings.
- Invite at least one therapist and one client.

## Day 1–3: Assignments + Review
- Assign 1–3 library items per client.
- Confirm client responses are received.
- Ensure therapists review responses in the Review Queue.

## Day 4–5: First AER
- Generate AER for at least one client over a 7-day period.
- Export the AER bundle (JSON + PDF + verification.txt).
- Verify bundle integrity locally using the public verification script.

## Day 6–7: Supervisor Review + Export
- Conduct supervisor review using AER artifacts.
- Share AER bundle with internal compliance or UR teams.
- Log feedback and confirm the verification process is understood.

## Success Indicators
- At least one reviewed response per client.
- AER bundle verifies PASS.
- Review cycle time decreases over the week.
