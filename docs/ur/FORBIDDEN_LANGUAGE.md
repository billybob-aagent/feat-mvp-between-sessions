# Forbidden Language (Do Not Use)

These phrases are not allowed in UR submissions because they imply unverified outcomes or decisioning.

- “AER determines medical necessity.”
- “AER is an AI decision or diagnosis.”
- “AER guarantees outcomes or adherence.”
- “AER replaces clinical judgment.”
- “Payer approval is implied by this report.”
- “Compliance is proven beyond doubt.”
- “This report is a medical necessity engine.”
