# UR Submission Email Templates

Use neutral, factual language. Do not imply outcomes, diagnosis, or payer endorsement.

## Generic Submission
Subject: AER Submission – <Clinic> – <Client> – <Period>

Body:
- Attached is the AER bundle for the requested period.
- The bundle contains JSON, PDF, and verification.txt for integrity checks.
- Missing data is explicitly labeled; no evidence is inferred.

## IOP Submission
Subject: IOP AER Submission – <Clinic> – <Client> – <Period>

Body:
- This submission supports IOP oversight for a seven-day period.
- Evidence is deterministic and based on recorded assignments, responses, and review actions.
- AER does not determine medical necessity.
